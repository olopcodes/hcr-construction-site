The story of heights

The team is comprised of a head constructor, designer, and engineer who grew up together in the neighborhood and desire to contribute positively to community. A day flipping through channels led one of the founders to a home improvement show, and the idea of Heights C and R began.

Using our knowledge in our respective professions we pooled our talents together and started the company. Many completions, and accolades later, we have established Heights as the primer destination for home improvement needs in the city
